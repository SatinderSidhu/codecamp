My Top 3 Most Favorite Rides at Dorney Park

3. Thunder Canyon

Thunder Canyon was a pretty fun ride. I thought it was a little uncomfortable when the water

was hitting hard on my head. It was basically that you go under different

waterfalls. Although that it was really rough, I still would confidently say that it

was my third favorite ride.


2. Monster

Monster was a really fun ride I did with Tegh. I loved the feeling in my stomach and the

view from the top was splendid! The only thing I didn't like was that it was boring when

being stranded on the top and waiting for the other people to go off the ride. Even though

that was boring, the view was beautiful, so I can say that it was my second favorite ride!!
